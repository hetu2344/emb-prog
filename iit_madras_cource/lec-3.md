## Microcontroller families
- Different types of familes like ESP, arduino, ARM and more
- ARM: Advance RISC Machine (RISC: Reduced Instruction Set Compiled)
- ARM is the most widely used in the day-to-day world
- Different types of cortext series ARM chips: A chip for application series (for OS), R chips for real times usage (these are fast and example used is in safety critical applications), M chips for microcontroller
